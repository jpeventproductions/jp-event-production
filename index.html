    Meet the Founder

    J Pierson

    J Pierson is a Nashville-based audio professional, DJ, event producer, and community builder with a Recording Industry degree from Middle Tennessee State University. His work connects live sound, DJ service, creative programming, and practical event planning for rooms that need to feel polished without becoming overbuilt.

    Through JP Event Production, J brings the same hands-on approach he has used across music showcases, community programs, school production sessions, producer events, and venue operations: understand the room, build the right coverage, keep the setup clean, and make the event feel ready from the first announcement to the last song.

    Room-first sound. Clean execution. Built around the event.
