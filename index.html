    Meet the Founder

    J Pierson

    J Pierson is a Nashville-based audio professional, DJ, event producer, and community builder with a Recording Industry degree from Middle Tennessee State University. His work connects live sound, DJ service, creative programming, and practical event planning for rooms that need to feel polished without becoming overbuilt.

    As a working DJ and production lead, J DJs 50+ weddings and events a year and that number continues to grow. His experience spans wedding receptions, private events, corporate rooms, community showcases, school production sessions, live music events, and venue operations.

    Through JP Event Production, J brings a hands-on, room-first approach: understand the venue, build the right coverage, keep the setup clean, communicate clearly, and make the event feel ready from the first announcement to the last song.
